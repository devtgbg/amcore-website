Choosing A Fit

Think about usage, not just voltage.

The best battery choice depends on how often the vehicle is used, how long it needs to run between charges, and how demanding the daily workload is.

  • Smaller packs suit lighter daily use and compact installations.
  • Larger packs suit longer runtime needs and heavier operating schedules.
  • Higher-voltage systems are better matched to 76V vehicle platforms.
